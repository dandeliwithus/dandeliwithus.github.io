Adrenaline filled adventure Activities in Dandeli

RAFTING:

The Rafting trail stretches for around 9.4km, Making it one of the most exciting and exhilirating stretch of rafting. The rapids grade 2 and grade 3 rapids. It takes around 4 hours to complete.

Coracle Ride:

The coracle is a small, roundish shaped, lightweight boat traditionally used. Although these boats were originally designed for general transport, They have recently been used mostly for giving tourists rides

Boating:

Boating is the leisurely activity of travelling by boat, It is a popular activity, and there are millions of boaters worldwide.

Kayaking:

Kayaking is the use of a kayak for moving across water. A kayak is a low-to-the-water-canoe-like boat in which the paddler sit facing forward legs in front, using double bladed paddle to pull front-to-back.

Trekking:

Trekking means a journey to be undertaken on foot in areas where motorised transport is generally not available. Trekking always refers to walking on trails, Trekking is not mountaineering but just jungle walking, It is a healthy activity.

Roller Zorbing:

A water roller or water walking roller is a large inflatable cylinder that allows a person inside it to walk across the surface of a body of water. The water roller is similar to the zorb , but it has only one layer and is designed for water travel rather than down-hill rolling.

Zip Line :

A zip line consists of a pulley suspended on a cable, usually made of stainless steel, mounted on a slope. It is designed to enable cargo or a person propelled by gravity to travel from the top to the bottom of the inclined cable by holding on to or being attached to the freely moving pulley.

Camp Fire:

A campfire is a fire at a campsite that provides light and warmth, and heat for cooking. It can also serve as a beacon, and an insect and predator deterrent.

Bird Watching:

Birdwatching is a form of wildlife observations in which the observation of birds is a recreational activity. It can be done with the naked eye by listening for bird sounds, or by watching with binoculars.
